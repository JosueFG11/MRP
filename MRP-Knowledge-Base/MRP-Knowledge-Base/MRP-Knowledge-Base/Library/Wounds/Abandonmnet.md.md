# Abandonment

## Definition
Fear of being left or unseen, stemming from inconsistent caregiving. This core wound fosters beliefs that connections are fragile and unreliable.

## Common Symptoms
- [[Trust-Issues]]: Skepticism toward others' reliability.
- [[Separation-Anxiety]]: Intense fear during separations.
- [[Clinginess-in-Relationships]]: Excessive attachment to prevent loss.
- [[Avoidance-of-Intimacy]]: Emotional walls to avoid vulnerability.
- [[Low-Self-Worth]]: Deep sense of unworthiness.
- [[Hypervigilance]]: Constant alertness for abandonment cues.
- [[Rejection-Sensitivity]]: Heightened reaction to perceived slights.

## Root Causes
Inconsistent parental presence, early losses, or emotional unavailability.
